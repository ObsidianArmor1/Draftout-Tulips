About & How to Use

  • This map contains every tulip patch in a square covering 40,000 blocks around spawn.
  • Tulips, useful for suspicious stew with the weakness effect, can generally be found in flower forests. In some seeds those aren’t easily available near spawn, making them hard to acquire. Rarely, patches of them will spawn in plains biomes and sunflower plains biomes. Tulip patches in plains are consistent between worlds as they ignore the world seed.
  • Purity is the percent of flowers in the patch that are guaranteed to be tulips when bonemealed!
  • Use F3+C to copy your coordinates, then press Ctrl + V (or Cmd + V) anywhere on this page to find the closest patches!
